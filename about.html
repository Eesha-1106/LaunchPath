--About--

LaunchPath is your all-in-one companion for turning ideas into impactful startups. Whether you're a student, a dreamer, or a budding entrepreneur, this platform simplifies the chaotic journey of building a startup into a structured, guided experience. We understand that starting up can feel overwhelming — which idea to choose, how to validate it, where to find tools, what steps to take, and how to avoid common pitfalls. That’s why StartUp-Deck is built to walk you through each phase — from identifying your problem to launching your MVP — using our 7-step framework. For every stage, we provide curated resources, free templates, video explainers, success stories, and even real-world challenges faced by founders. Let’s build something amazing, one step at a time.
